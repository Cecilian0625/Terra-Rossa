Cold Brew Coffee: Refreshing and Smooth

Cold brew coffee is a unique beverage that extracts coffee flavor through a long, cold water brewing process. Unlike traditional hot water-extracted coffee, cold brew is favored for its low acidity and smooth taste.

The process of making cold brew coffee is simple but requires patience. Coarsely ground coffee is steeped in cold water for 12 to 24 hours, then filtered to separate the coffee liquid. Without the use of hot water, this method preserves the original flavor of the coffee beans and reduces bitterness.

Cold brew coffee is perfect for summer, enjoyed over ice or with added milk or syrup. Its low acidity and distinct flavor make it an excellent choice for cooling down and quenching your thirst.At our caf√©, we select the finest coffee beans and meticulously craft each cup of cold brew.
